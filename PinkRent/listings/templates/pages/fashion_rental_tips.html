{% trans "Ask the lender to outline all costs, including the rental price, deposit, and any insurance fees. Carefully read through the terms and ask questions if anything is unclear." %}

{% trans "If you are concerned about the fit, discuss possible solutions with the lender. You may request a fitting option, such as a 24-hour try-on period, where you receive the item, try it on, and send it back immediately under agreed conditions." %}

{% trans "Clarify Additional Costs" %}

{% trans "Ask if there are any extra costs included in the rental period, such as shipping and cleaning. Make sure all costs are transparent before finalizing the rental." %}

{% trans "Once you receive the item, treat it with utmost care. If any issues arise during the rental period (e.g., loose stitch or broken zipper), inform the lender immediately." %}

{% trans "Discuss Wear and Tear" %}

{% trans "Before the rental period, discuss potential outcomes for wear and tear situations with the lender to avoid misunderstandings." %}
